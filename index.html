Frothy Makeup Looks Inspired by In-season Fall Produce
Frothy released its first issue focused on food in October. The images shown on this site are featured in a story on eating fruits and vegetables that are in season. The team created recipes based on various produce items and styled three outfits inspired by the recipes.
